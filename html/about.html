IEPF stands for Investor Education and Protection Fund. If shares or dividends remain unclaimed for seven consecutive years, they are transferred to the IEPF. RIAPL specializes in the complex legal process of reclaiming these assets for the rightful owners.

The timeline varies depending on the company's Registrar and Transfer Agent (RTA) and the completeness of documentation. Typically, it takes anywhere from 4 to 8 weeks. Our team audits your documents upfront to minimize delays.

Share transmission (in case of death) requires documents such as the original share certificates, notarized copy of the death certificate, succession certificate (or probate/will), and KYC documents of the legal heirs. We provide a tailored checklist for your specific case.
