Our Services

We provide specialized end-to-end solutions for all your physical share related needs.

Physical to Demat Conversion

  • Expert guidance on converting physical shares to demat form
  • Coordinating with RTAs for the right process and documentation
  • Compliance with SEBI regulations for seamless dematerialization

IEPF Claims Recovery

  • Expert assistance in claiming back shares and bonds transferred to IEPF
  • Complete documentation support and liaison with authorities
  • Streamlined process management from application to receipt of securities

Transmission of Shares

  • Smooth transfer of shares to legal heirs after the demise of shareholders
  • Comprehensive documentation and legal support
  • Coordination with Registrars and Transfer Agents (RTAs)

Issuance of Duplicate Share Certificates

  • Replacement of lost physical share certificates
  • End-to-end support including indemnity bond preparation
  • Coordination with companies and RTAs for swift issuance

Name Correction & Deletion

  • Rectification of name-related errors in share certificates
  • Deletion of deceased co-holders' names
  • Ensuring accurate records for hassle-free transactions

Other Specialized Services

  • Change of address and bank details in shareholder records
  • Guidance for form filling & documentation
  • Corporate action advisory and support
